ICC WOMEN'S T20 WORLD CUP

YOU DONT PLAY FOR THE CROWD YOU PLAY FOR THE COUNTRY


The ICC Women's T20 World Cup is the biennial international championship for women's Twenty20 International cricket..

The event is organised by the sport's governing body, the International Cricket Council (ICC)

At each tournament, a set number of teams qualify automatically, with the remaining teams

The qualified teams will be decided by the women t20 qualifier.

Australia, having won the tournament six times, are the most successful team.
