POWERFUL PEOPLE COME FROM POWERFUL PLACES


The makers of NBK 108, a forthcoming film starring Nandamuri Balakrishna in the lead role

released the first look poster on social media on Wednesday to mark the occasion of Ugadi.

The next film, NBK 108, is directed by Anil Ravipudi and produced by Shine Screens'.

The poster features Balakrishna in two different avatars, one in traditional attire with a salt-and-pepper look

and the other in an aggressive look with a beard and handlebar moustache.

Balakrishna adorned with holy threads on his neck and hand, along with a tattoo inked on his hand.

The first look poster of NBK 108 has generated a buzz on social media, with fans eagerly waiting for the film's release
