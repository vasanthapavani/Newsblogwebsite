The Telugu remake of Vinodhaya Sitham, starring Pawan Kalyan and Sai Dharam Tej, is set to start its shoot

This will be Pawan Kalyan's first time sharing screen space with his nephew Sai Dharam Tej

The film is directed by Samuthirakani, an actor-director who is remaking his Tamil film 'Vinodaya Sitham' in Telugu.

Pawan Kalyan will reprise his role as Samuthirakani (God of Time) in the Telugu remake

Tej will play a man who is involved in an accident and is given a second chance at life.
