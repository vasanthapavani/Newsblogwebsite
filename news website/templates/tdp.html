TELUGU DESAM PARTY

'PEOPLE FIRST' IS OUR MANTRA


The Telugu Desam Party is an Indian regional political party with great influence in the states of Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

It was founded by the former chief minister of Andhra Pradesh N. T. Rama Rao (N.T.R.) on 29 March 1982

MLC stands for members of state legislative assembly having a tenure of six years and should be a citizen of India.

One twelfth are elected by persons who are graduates of three years' standing residing in that state.
