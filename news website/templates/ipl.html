The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a men's T20 franchise cricket league in India.

It is annually contested by ten teams based in seven cities and three states.

The league was founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) in 2007.

Brijesh Patel is the incumbent chairman of IPL.

It is usually held annually in summer across India between March to May.

The most awaited T20 cricket tournament is only a month away.

Indian Premier League 2023 will commence on March 31

The reigning champions, Chennai Super Kings and Gujarat Titans, will lock horns in the first match
