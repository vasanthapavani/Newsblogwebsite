It took around 19 months for the superhit musical number Naatu Naatu which won a historic Oscar for Best Original Song

A track from Telugu blockbuster RRR, Naatu Naatu was the first Indian film song to be nominated for an Oscar.

Composed by MM Keeravani with lyrics penned by Chandrabose,already made historywhen it won the Golden Globe for best original song,

Featuring superstars Ram Charan and Jr NTR in lead roles, RRR tells the fictional story of fight against the British

Rajamouli says he envisioned Naatu Naatu as a "fight scene" in which two freedom fighters bring a British officer to his knees - through dance.

The song culminates in a marathon dance-off as all the dancers kick up a storm, slowly collapsing one by one in exhaustion until only the heroes remain standing.
